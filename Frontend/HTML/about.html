Welcome to Rohit's Cougar Cafe UHSL! We're excited to announce the recent opening of our new establishment in University of Houston, SugarLand campus.

Our Mission
At Rohit's Cougar Cafe, our mission is to provide tasty and healthy meals to the students and staff at an affordable cost.

How we operate
Locally Sourced: We prioritize and procure fresh, locally sourced ingredients to support our community and deliver the highest quality food.
Quality food: Serving quality food is always our priority.
Environmental Sustainability: Rohit's Cougar Cafe is committed to eco-friendly practices.
Our Staff: Behind every extraordinary dish is an extraordinary team. Our staffs are well-trained and knowledgeable about the menu. They should be able to provide recommendations, and accommodate special dietary needs.
